INTERSTELLARA

Making learning in space as easy as ABC

Interstellara is a space program that gives African American students an opportunity to go to space for free. Interstellara also gives students a chance to witness life on Mars, Jupiter and a surprise trip to VENUS. Students need to have a GPA of 2.3 or higher and there are no age limits. SEE YOU IN SPACE
